Scientific Reference Collection

Reference
Objects

The Atlas is based on a unique reference collection of authentic ancient Chinese ceramics and porcelain. Each object included in this collection has undergone comprehensive scholarly investigation.

Every reference object has received professional art-historical attribution, has been examined by recognised specialists in Chinese ceramics and works of art, and has also undergone instrumental diagnostic research, including macro- and microstructural analysis.

Each object is supported by a comprehensive body of scientific documentation, including high-resolution photography, microscopic observations, diagnostic descriptions, and a series of peer-discussed scientific publications available in open access.

These reference objects constitute the scientific foundation of the Atlas and provide reliable comparative material for researchers, museum professionals, conservators, collectors, and specialists in Chinese ceramics.

Published reference objects

RO-MING-JDZ-001 Confirmed

Ming Dynasty · 明朝

Imperial Sacrificial Yellow Bottle Vase with Dragon and Phoenix

Chenghua Period · 1464–1487

A principal reference object for the study of Chenghua imperial porcelain. Documents the characteristic yellow sacrificial glaze, natural age-related crackle, and underglaze cobalt reign mark as diagnostic standards.
